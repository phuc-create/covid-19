All the things you should know about Corona Virus (SAR-COVID - COVID 19)

What is Covid ?
Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by a newly discovered coronavirus.
What will happen ?
Most people infected with the COVID-19 virus will experience mild to moderate respiratory illness and recover without requiring special treatment. Older people, and those with underlying medical problems like cardiovascular disease, diabetes, chronic respiratory disease, and cancer are more likely to develop serious illness.

- PROTECT YOURSELF AND PEOPLE AROUND -
Face mask
Wear fabric face masks in public and crowded places,wear medical face masks at health facilities quarantine camps
Disinfection
Wash hands redularly with soap, hand sanitiser, disinfect surface/items frequently touched, keep house clean and airy.
Distance
Keep distance when making contacts with others
Health Declarations
When experiencing symptoms of fever,coughing, shortness of breath, dial Ministry of Health's hotline 19009095
No Gatherings
Limit gatherings in groups in crowded places to avoid unfortunate infections
